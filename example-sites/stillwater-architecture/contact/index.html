The first five months

How we start a project.

  1. 01

    A first conversation

    A 45-minute call or studio visit, no charge. We listen, ask questions about your site and the life you want to lead in the building. You leave with a sense of whether we are the right practice for the project, and we with a sense of whether the project is right for us.

  2. 02

    A site walk

    One or both partners visit the site with you. Two to three hours, usually including a coffee afterward. No drawings are produced at this stage — we are only trying to understand the place and the people.

  3. 03

    An engagement letter

    A short written proposal: scope, stages, fees, timeline. No obligation to sign. Typically delivered within two weeks of the site walk.

  4. 04

    Design begins

    Once the engagement letter is signed and the first-stage deposit received, we begin the brief and preliminary design. From here the project follows our standard five-stage process.

  5. 05

    We build it

    A builder is selected, contract signed, and we spend the next 14–24 months on fortnightly site visits until the building is handed over. And then we come back to see it in its first winter.

One last note

We only take on projects where we think we can improve someone’s life for the next thirty years. If that sounds like what you’re looking for, we would like to hear from you.
