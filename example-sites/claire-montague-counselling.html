Hi, I'm Claire.

I'm a registered counsellor based in Wollongong, working with individuals, couples and families. I believe everyone deserves a safe, non-judgemental space to talk about the things that weigh on them.

My practice is built on warmth, empathy and a belief that people have the capacity to grow and change — sometimes they just need the right support.

Why I became a counsellor.

I came to counselling after a career change. I'd always been the person friends and family turned to, and eventually I realised that helping people work through difficult things was what I wanted to do with my life.

I went back to study, completed my qualifications, and built a private practice here in Wollongong. I work with people from all walks of life — individuals navigating anxiety, couples trying to reconnect, families going through transitions.

What I offer isn't advice or quick fixes. It's a space to be heard, to make sense of what you're experiencing, and to find a way forward that feels right for you.

My approach.

I draw on a range of evidence-based modalities and tailor my approach to each person. There's no rigid framework — we work together to find what's most helpful.

Sessions are warm, conversational and confidential. Some people come with a specific issue. Others just need space to talk. Both are welcome.

I offer sessions in person at my Wollongong practice, and via secure video call for those who prefer telehealth.

How I can help.

I offer individual, couples and family counselling — in person in Wollongong or via telehealth. Here's what each looks like.

Individual Counselling

One-on-one sessions in a confidential, non-judgemental space. We work at your pace on whatever matters most to you — whether that's something specific or a general sense that things aren't quite right.

Sessions are conversational, warm and guided by your goals. There's no rigid structure.

Common areas:
AnxietyStressSelf-esteemGriefLife transitionsBurnout

Couples Counselling

A safe, neutral space for both partners to feel heard. Whether you're navigating conflict, rebuilding trust, or simply wanting to communicate better — we work together to understand the patterns and find a path forward.

Both people are equally supported. The goal isn't to assign blame — it's to build understanding.

Common areas:
CommunicationConflictTrustReconnectionSeparation support

Family Counselling

When family relationships feel strained — between parents and children, siblings, blended families, or extended family — sessions focus on improving communication and understanding each other's perspectives.

Common areas:
ParentingBlended familiesAdolescent issuesSeparation & co-parenting

Telehealth Sessions

All services are available via secure video call. Telehealth sessions work exactly the same as in-person — just from wherever you're comfortable. Ideal if you're outside Wollongong, have mobility issues, or prefer the flexibility.
