Before you ask

The usual questions.

Is there a lock-in contract?
No. You can pause, skip or cancel any week — just let us know by SMS. We ask for a heads-up before noon the day prior so we can re-route.
What does "from $45" actually mean?
The $45 rate applies to standard 300m² blocks in our regular Shire rotation. Larger blocks, steep slopes, or extra-long grass on first-time mows get a fixed quote before we start — always in writing, never a surprise.
Do you charge extra for edging?
No. Full edging and whipper-snipping are included in every single mow, every single visit. If your previous mower was charging extra for it, you were being had.
What about green waste?
Clippings from weekly/fortnightly mows stay on-site (we blow them off hard surfaces and mulch them into the lawn). For cleanups, hedging and landscaping jobs, full removal is quoted up-front.
What if it rains?
We push to the next dry day and SMS you first. Rain or wet grass isn't good for the lawn or the mower blades, so we'd rather reschedule than rush it.
Do you use weedkillers or chemicals?
Minimally, and never without asking first. Spot spraying of bindii and clover is offered as an extra in spring. We don't blanket-spray lawns and we won't use glyphosate near edible gardens.
How do I pay?
Invoice by email after each visit (or monthly if you're on a regular plan). Card, bank transfer or PayID — whichever suits. Regular clients can set up a standing order through their bank.
Are you insured?
Yes — full public liability to $20M, plus personal indemnity. Certificates available on request. Every team member is also police-checked.
