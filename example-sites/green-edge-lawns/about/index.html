About the mob

Three Shire locals with a trailer, a sharp blade and a plan.

Green Edge started in 2018 with one Honda push-mower in the back of Jake’s 1998 Hilux, and a list of seven regular clients his mum’s book-club had rounded up. Seven years on we’re still small on purpose — three of us, thirty kilometres of Shire, every week.

No franchises. No sub-contractors. If you booked Green Edge, it’s Green Edge at your gate.

The story

From the Cronulla Golf greens to Caringbah front yards.

Jake grew up in Gymea, finished school at Port Hacking High in 2012, and went straight into a turf management apprenticeship at Cronulla Golf Club. Four years of 5am starts taught him what a healthy lawn actually looks like — and the ten or so mistakes most home-owners make that quietly kill them.

He went out on his own in 2018 after a mate asked him to mow his front yard for cash on a Saturday. That mate’s neighbour asked next, then the neighbour’s sister in Miranda. By Christmas he had 34 regulars and a second-hand trailer.

Mitch joined in late 2022 as a Tuesday-Thursday casual while he finished his Hort Cert at TAFE Loftus. Sam came on in 2023 to run the landscaping side after he quit carpentry. The three of them now cover about 90 regular clients plus one-offs, and still knock off in time for a surf most afternoons.

How we work

Four things we don’t compromise on.

01

We turn up.

On the day we said, in the window we said. If we're going to be late, you get an SMS before we were due to arrive — not after.

02

Sharp blades, short visits.

A dull blade rips grass and stresses a lawn. Blades get sharpened every Friday night. Most front-and-back jobs take us 25-35 minutes.

03

No blanket chemicals.

Spot-spray for bindii, sure. Blanket-spraying a family's lawn with glyphosate? No. If a lawn is sick, we aerate, top-dress and overseed first.

04

Every load of green waste goes to compost.

Certified facility at Kurnell — never to landfill. You don't pay more for it, it's just how we operate.
