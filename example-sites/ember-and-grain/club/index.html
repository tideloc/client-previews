Frequently asked

Small print, plain language.

When does my box arrive? +

We pack the first Tuesday of every month. Local pickup (Federal NSW) from the Wednesday. Postage goes out Thursday — most of NSW gets it by the weekend, most of Australia inside a week.

Can I pause or skip a month? +

Yes. You can pause indefinitely, skip a single month, or change your address or tier any time up to 3 days before pack day. Everything lives in your members area — one email to reset your password and you're in.

What's 'brew deck' and why do I care? +

The brew deck is our 200L second system. We use it for mixed-culture, barrel-aged and one-off beers that never go near the main kit. Members on Serious + Unhinged get a bottle in every box; those bottles don't leave the taproom otherwise.

Do you ship beer interstate? +

Yes, across Australia. We don't ship to WA in January/February (heat spoils the beer in transit) — we'll pause those members' boxes and resume in March without any lost months.

Can I gift a membership? +

Yes. Three, six, or twelve month gift runs. It comes with a hand-stamped letter from the brewers and a taproom tasting voucher. The recipient can swap tiers after the gift period ends.

Is there a commitment period? +

No. Month-to-month, cancel any time, no term. If you cancel after less than three months we will ask (quickly and politely) why, because it helps us do the club better. That's it.

Ready to join?

Sign up before the 25th to make the next month's box. Pay monthly, cancel whenever, no term. We'll be glad to have you.
