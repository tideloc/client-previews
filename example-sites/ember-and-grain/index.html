The taproom

A twelve-metre bar
under the camphor laurels.

We built the taproom into the old truss shed on the corner of Federal Drive and Eltham Lane — high ceilings, enamel signs, cold concrete floor, one very good sound system. Twelve taps, a short wine list, a different food truck every night we're open.

Kids welcome until 7pm, dogs welcome all the time, bookings not essential but recommended on Saturdays.

From the pairing pad

What to drink with
duck fat potatoes.

The answer is not wine. Duck fat potatoes — properly done, parboiled, shaken, then roasted at 220°C until the edges go dark — want a beer with enough malt depth to meet the fat and enough carbonation to scrub the palate. We pour two things with them.

01 · The obvious

Federal Dark

The cold-toast-and-cocoa of the mild picks up the roasty edge of the potato crust without fighting with rosemary or garlic.

02 · The better answer

Field Pale

Passionfruit-pith carbonation + crisp malt spine cuts the duck fat clean, doesn't over-talk the herbs.
