About Matt.

A local Newcastle electrician who started his own business because he believed homeowners deserved better.

Started because Newcastle deserved a sparky who gives a damn.

Matt started his apprenticeship as a teenager and worked his way through the trade with commercial and residential outfits across the Hunter Valley.

After years of hearing the same complaints from friends and family — tradies not showing up, surprise invoices, sloppy work — he decided to go out on his own and do things differently.

The idea behind Matt Lawson Electrical is straightforward: answer the phone, show up when you say you will, charge what you quote, and do the work properly.

Today, most of the work comes through word of mouth and repeat customers. That's the best kind of advertising — it means the work speaks for itself.
