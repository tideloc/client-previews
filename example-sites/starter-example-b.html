About Paws & Suds

Since 2018, Paws & Suds has been the trusted choice for pet grooming on the South Coast. Our experienced groomers treat every pet like family, ensuring they leave looking and feeling their absolute best. We use only premium, pet-safe products and provide a calm, welcoming environment where your furry friends can relax and enjoy their spa day.
