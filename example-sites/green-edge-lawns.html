About GreenEdge

GreenEdge started in 2021 with a ute, a mower, and a goal: do good work for good people at a fair price. We're a small crew of three based in Lake Macquarie, and every property we work on gets the same attention to detail whether it's a townhouse courtyard or a five-acre block.

We're not a franchise. We don't send different people every week. When you book GreenEdge, you get us — the same crew, same quality, every time. We show up on time, we do what we say we'll do, and we clean up after ourselves.

Most of our work comes from regular clients across Charlestown, Belmont, Warners Bay, Swansea, and surrounding suburbs. If your yard needs sorting out, chances are we've already worked on your neighbour's.
