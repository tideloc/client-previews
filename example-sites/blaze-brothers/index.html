Two brothers. One smoker.

Started at the markets.
Never left.

Dan & Mick Blaze set up a trestle table at the Jan Powers Farmers Market in March 2019 with twenty bottles of Widowmaker and a hand-chalked price sign. They sold out by 9:30am.

Five years on, they still bottle everything themselves in a tin shed off Montague Rd — just with more shelves, a proper smoker, and a queue at every market they show up to.
