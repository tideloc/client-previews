Questions we get asked

FAQ.

Do I need to commit? +

No. Skip, pause, or cancel any time from your account — no emails, no retention calls. Changes before Monday 9am apply to that week's roast.

Can I choose the coffee? +

You pick a stream: Espresso-leaning, Filter-leaning, or Roaster's Choice. Within that we rotate single origins quarterly — each bag ships with a tasting note so you know what's arrived.

When do bags ship? +

We roast Mon / Wed / Fri. Your bag ships the day after its roast date — so your coffee has rested on the shelf before it's in the post.

Where do you ship? +

Australia-wide. 1-4 business days via Australia Post Express. Free shipping on all subscription orders.

Whole bean or ground? +

Default is whole bean. You can switch to a specific grind (espresso, moka, filter, plunger) per delivery — change it in your dashboard.

What if I go on holiday? +

Log in and pause. Resume any time. We don't auto-charge a paused account.
