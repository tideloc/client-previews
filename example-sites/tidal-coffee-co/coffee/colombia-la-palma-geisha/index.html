Origin story

From Tolima.

We bought this at auction in March — 32kg, one sack, SCA score 87.5 from Elías Roa's farm in Planadas. Washed Geisha, 72-hour fermentation, shaded patio dry. We're releasing it only as 150g tins at the cafe and online until it runs out. Brew it V60, 1:17, 94°C, one gentle agitation. Drink it black. Don't talk.

Grind guide

Grind to your gear.

Buying whole bean? Every home grinder is different. These are our starting points — if your cup is weak, go one finer; if it's bitter, go one coarser.

If we grind for you, tell us your method.

Method Grind Feels like
Espresso Very fine Table salt — adjust by second
Aeropress Medium-fine Between espresso and filter
V60 / Chemex Medium-fine to medium Rough sand
Moka pot Fine Finer than filter, coarser than espresso
French Press Coarse Raw sugar, uneven is fine
