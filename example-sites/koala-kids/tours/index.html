What to expect

Forty minutes, four beats.

  1. 01

    A proper welcome

    Harriet or Liam meets you at reception with a cup of tea. We want to know you and your child before we walk into any room.

  2. 02

    All five rooms

    We walk through each room in age order. You'll see the program in action and meet the lead educator on duty.

  3. 03

    The garden & bush kinder site

    Weather permitting, we finish in the garden. Bush kinder site is a short drive — we'll show you photos if you can't come to us.

  4. 04

    Your questions, not our pitch

    The second half is yours. We answer anything. We also give you a one-page summary of fees for your specific days.

Tour FAQ

Before you come.

Can I bring my child?
Please do. Seeing your child in the environment is half the point. We build the tour around their pace.
Can partners attend?
Absolutely. We encourage both carers to visit, whenever it works for you.
How long does a tour take?
Around 40 minutes. Longer if you have questions — we never rush.
What if I can't make a tour time?
Call us. We occasionally run Monday-evening tours for shift-working parents.
Do you do virtual tours?
Yes — by appointment, conducted by Harriet over video call. Not a pre-recorded walkthrough.
