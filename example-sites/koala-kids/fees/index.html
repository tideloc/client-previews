Fee FAQ

Things parents ask us.

Are you CCS approved?
Yes. Koala Kids holds a full Service Approval through the Department of Education and is registered for the Child Care Subsidy. We'll help you complete your CCS application during enrolment.
Do you require a bond or holding fee?
A $250 refundable bond is payable on enrolment, fully refunded on your child's last day (or forfeited if notice periods are not met). No other upfront fees.
What's the notice period?
Two weeks' written notice for any permanent schedule change or withdrawal.
Do I pay for public holidays?
Yes. Fees apply for all booked days, including public holidays. This is standard under the CCS framework.
Do you offer any fee relief?
We hold two Additional Child Care Subsidy places for families experiencing temporary financial hardship. Speak with Harriet in confidence.
