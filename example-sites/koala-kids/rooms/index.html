Five rooms

From first steps to the year before school.

Our rooms are grouped by stage, not chronological age. Ratios are better than the national minimum. Each room has two primary educators who stay with the cohort all year.

0–1 years

Joey Room

Ratio 1:4

A calm, slow-paced nursery where routines are led by the baby, not the clock. Responsive care, secure attachment, soft sensory play.

Read more →
1–2 years

Wombat Room

Ratio 1:5

Little walkers, big feelings. Our toddlers move a lot, feel a lot, and begin to find their words. We make space for all of it.

Read more →
2–3 years

Possum Room

Ratio 1:8

Project-based, messy, loud. Two-year-olds are our most creative philosophers. We follow their questions wherever they lead.

Read more →
3–4 years

Wallaby Room

Ratio 1:10

Where friendships form and curiosity sharpens. We lean into early literacy, nature inquiry, and the beginnings of group belonging.

Read more →
4–5 years

Kookaburra Preschool

Ratio 1:11

The year before big school. A rich, rigorous preschool program grounded in our bush kinder site, with strong transition-to-school support.
