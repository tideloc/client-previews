Developmental focus

Three things we watch for, document, and talk with you about.

Focus 01

Early literacy through rich oral language

Focus 02

Friendship skills, conflict repair, negotiation

Focus 03

Sustained inquiry over multi-day projects

Parent handover

You hear from us daily.

Every afternoon, the lead educator in Wallaby Room gives each family a two-minute verbal handover at pickup — what your child ate, how they slept, what they played with, and one specific moment from their day. Written learning stories come home weekly through our parent portal.

For new families, Sarah and the team run a bespoke settling-in programme across the first two weeks. No one is dropped into a full day on day one.
