THE FULL STORY

It started the way most good things do — with a dare. Nick bet his brother Sam that he couldn't eat an entire Scorpion pepper raw. Sam did. Then got obsessed with growing them. Two years later they had rows of Carolina Reapers, Ghosts, Habaneros, and Trinidad Scorpions stretching across a patch of dirt in the Lockyer Valley.

The sauces came next. Fermenting, blending, bottling in the kitchen. Giving jars to mates. Taking a table at the local farmers market "just to see." They sold out in two hours. That was 2020. Since then, Blaze Brothers has grown from 5 sauces to 14 products, from one market to four, and from a kitchen bench to a proper commercial setup — but every batch is still cooked by Nick and Sam.

The philosophy hasn't changed: grow the best chillies we can, source the rest from Australian farms, use zero preservatives, and make sauces with genuine flavour at every heat level. Whether you're a gentle-entry beginner or an extreme-only veteran, there's something in the lineup for you.
