Frequently Asked Questions

What are your membership options? +

We offer monthly memberships starting at $49, annual memberships with discounts, and day passes at $15. All memberships include access to all classes and facilities.

Do you offer personal training? +

Yes, we offer personalized training sessions with our certified trainers. Sessions can be booked at $60 per hour. We also offer packages with discounts.

Can I try a class before joining? +

Absolutely! First-time visitors can try any class free of charge. We offer a complimentary tour of the facility and a free introductory session.

What are your hours of operation? +

We're open Mon-Fri 5:00 AM - 10:00 PM, Saturday 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM, and Sunday 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM. 24-hour access available for premium members.
