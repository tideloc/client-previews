Wild Macadamia

Macadamia integrifolia

Price
$34
Size
300g jar
Harvest
Autumn · Mar–Jun

Raw, unsalted macadamias from regenerated heritage groves on Bundjalung Country — a variety much closer to the wild tree than the commercial hybrid. Higher oil content, smaller kernel, more complex flavour. We buy only from growers who tree-fall harvest (no shakers), which protects the forest soil and the old trees.

1
Grown on Country

This Wild Macadamia was harvested on Bundjalung Country, NSW, and brought to us by Tweed Valley Rainforest Co-op. We pay our harvest partners a published per-kilo rate above market floor and work on three-year contracts.

Sustainability

Wild-harvested under the guidance of the cooperative Elders on a two- or three-year rotation. We buy a minimum floor volume each year regardless of demand so the harvesters can plan. Glass jars are locally sourced; labels are unbleached recycled stock.

Acknowledgment of Country
With respect.

Outback Pantry acknowledges the Arrernte people as the Traditional Custodians of the land on which our Alice Springs warehouse stands, and we pay our respects to Elders past and present.
