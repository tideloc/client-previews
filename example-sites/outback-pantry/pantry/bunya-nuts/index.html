Bunya Nuts · Shelled

Araucaria bidwillii

Price
$29
Size
200g pack
Harvest
Summer · Jan–Mar

Hand-gathered bunya nuts from the bunya pines of the Bunya Mountains — a tree that only produces a full crop every three years. Shelled, vacuum-sealed, ready to roast. Treat them like a cross between a chestnut and a waxy potato: boil, roast, or purée. Sacred food for Wakka Wakka and Jinibara people; we only buy during the formal harvest gatherings.

1
Grown on Country

This Bunya Nuts · Shelled was harvested on Wakka Wakka Country, QLD, and brought to us by Bunya Mountains Harvest Circle. We pay our harvest partners a published per-kilo rate above market floor and work on three-year contracts.

Sustainability

Wild-harvested under the guidance of the cooperative Elders on a two- or three-year rotation. We buy a minimum floor volume each year regardless of demand so the harvesters can plan. Glass jars are locally sourced; labels are unbleached recycled stock.
