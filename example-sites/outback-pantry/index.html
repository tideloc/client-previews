Acknowledgment of Country

The food on this shelf has been cared for for 60,000 years.

Outback Pantry is based in Mparntwe, on the unceded lands of the Arrernte people. We work with five Indigenous-led cooperatives across the Northern Territory, the Kimberley, Tasmania and Western NSW.

The knowledge that tells us a wattleseed is ready in August, that a quandong needs three seasons of rain to fruit, that a pepperberry can be split but never pounded — all of it was carried here by First Nations people across thousands of generations.

Outback Pantry is a settler-founded grocer. We are not Indigenous-owned, and we try not to pretend to be. What we can do is publish our payment rates, work on three-year contracts, and give you a product that lets you honour the Country it came from.

Acknowledgment of Country
With respect.

Outback Pantry acknowledges the Arrernte people as the Traditional Custodians of the land on which our Alice Springs warehouse stands, and we pay our respects to Elders past and present. Every ingredient in this pantry has been harvested from Country under the guidance of the First Nations cooperatives we partner with. The knowledge behind this food is 60,000 years old.
