// What to expect

Arrive at 9. Leave at 4.

0900 — Meet at the shed, tea & a safety walk-through. Aprons on.

0930 — First heat. You'll learn to light and tend the fire and to read steel colour. This is half the craft.

1100 — Drawing out. Tapering, upsetting, the fundamentals. We hit 20-30 heats together.

1230 — Lunch. Made by Sara, usually includes something from the oven. Tea strong.

1330 — Your project. Close one-on-one.

1600 — Cool your piece, wax it, walk out with it. Plus a workshop alumni discount on all orders for 12 months.

// Things to know

Boots, jeans,
long sleeves.

  • 01 /Wear closed leather boots, long jeans, and a long-sleeve cotton shirt. No synthetics — they melt.
  • 02 /Tie back long hair. Leather apron provided.
  • 03 /Minimum age is 16. Under-18s need a parent or guardian signed consent.
  • 04 /We recommend eating properly beforehand. It's a physical day.
  • 05 /Reschedule up to 14 days out for a full refund. After that it's a store credit or a transfer.
  • 06 /We can sign a tax-deductible receipt for professional development.
