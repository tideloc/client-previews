The Story Behind Koala Kids

Started by Parents, for Parents

Koala Kids was born in 2019 after our founders spent too many weekends hunting for decent party hire that didn't cost a fortune or show up looking rough.

We started with two bouncy castles and a trailer. Today we've got a full warehouse in Marrickville packed with party gear that we clean, maintain, and safety-check before every single hire.

FAQ

Common Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend 2 weeks ahead. During peak birthday season (Sep-Nov), 3-4 weeks is ideal.
Where do you deliver?
Free delivery across Inner West — Marrickville, Newtown, Ashfield, Dulwich Hill, Summer Hill, Leichhardt, and surrounds.
What if it rains?
Heavy rain = free reschedule. Light showers are fine. We don't operate in lightning or strong winds.
How does payment work?
30% deposit to secure. Balance due 48 hours before. Card, bank transfer, or PayID accepted.
