// Quick answers

Four questions we get every week.

How quickly can you get to an emergency callout?
Matt or one of the team is generally on the road within 60 minutes of your call for Wollongong, Port Kembla, Shellharbour and Figtree. After-hours callouts carry a fixed fee that's quoted upfront.

Do you give fixed prices or run on hourly rates?
For most small-to-medium residential jobs — switchboards, EV chargers, smoke alarms, lighting retrofits — we quote a fixed price after a site visit. Larger rewires and commercial fitouts are itemised line by line. You will never get a "number of hours" surprise.

Are you actually authorised for Level 2 meter work?
Yes. Matt holds Endeavour Energy Level 2 ASP authorisation, which means we can cut, upgrade and reconnect the service mains and meter board without waiting on a retailer. We handle all permit paperwork.

Do you offer a warranty on your work?
Five years on our workmanship, plus the manufacturer warranty on all hardware (panels, inverters, batteries, chargers, lighting). If anything goes wrong inside five years, we come back and fix it at no cost.
