Refill program · 2,400+ vessels refilled

Keep the vessel.
Refill slowly.

Every Saltbrush candle is built to be refilled. Return your empty vessel and we’ll clean it, re-wick it, and hand-pour a fresh candle into it for 30% less than a new one — in any signature scent you like.

Order a refill pouch Choose a scent

How it works

Four steps, zero fuss.

Send the vessel. Pick the scent. We’ll handle the rest.

01

Finish the candle

Let the last of the wax pool fully. Remove the wick clip when it cools.

02

Return your vessel

Drop it at the Hahndorf studio, or mail it back via reply-paid. Free for orders over $80.

03

We clean and re-wick

Every vessel is hand-washed, dried overnight, re-wicked, and prepared for a fresh pour.

04

Pour, cure, return

We pour the scent you chose, cure it for five days and ship it back. 30% off a new candle.

Pricing

30% less than a new candle.

  • 280g refill$38 (vs $58 new)
  • 140g refill$22 (vs $34 new)
  • Return postage$6 reply-paid, free over $80
  • Turnaround7–10 days from studio arrival
A few notes

The fine print.

  • · Any Saltbrush scent fits any Saltbrush vessel — mix freely.
  • · We refill each vessel up to three times. After that the ceramic tires and we retire it gracefully.
  • · Non-Saltbrush vessels can’t be refilled — we only pour into ceramic we know the thermal profile of.
  • · Cracked or chipped vessels aren’t refillable. If yours breaks in transit, we’ll replace it at half price.
