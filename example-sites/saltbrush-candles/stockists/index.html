Wholesale enquiries.

We work with a small, deliberate list of retailers, cafés, hotels and spas — most of whom have been with us for years. We open wholesale applications twice a year, in March and September, and review every application personally.

Minimum order is 12 units across the range, wholesale pricing at 55% of RRP, with standard 14-day payment terms. First orders ship with free POS and a hand-written introduction card.

  • Wholesale55% of RRP
