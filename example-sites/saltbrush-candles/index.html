Our story

A studio in a stone cottage, just off Main Street.

Saltbrush & Co. started in 2019 at Marni Clarke’s kitchen table, with one pot of melted soy wax and a gut feeling that people wanted scents that behaved like a real place, not a brief.

Five years on, every candle is still hand-poured in the old bluestone cottage on Hahndorf’s Main Street — now with better shelves, better labels, and a deep bench of native Australian fragrances that we’ve taken our sweet time to get right.

Four families

How we think about scent.

Every Saltbrush scent lives in one of four families. It’s a quiet way to help you pick — and a cheat-sheet for layering two together if you want to make your house smell like only your house.

Family
Native

Saltbrush, wattle, eucalyptus, honey myrtle.

Family
Citrus

Lemon myrtle, grapefruit peel, verbena.

Family
Woody

Sandalwood, ironbark, vetiver, leather.

Family
Floral

Boronia, pear blossom, elderflower, orris.

// Refill program · Batch 047

Keep the vessel.
Skip the waste.

Every Saltbrush candle is built to be refilled. Send your empty vessel back (or drop it off in Hahndorf, Stirling or Daylesford) and we’ll gently re-pour it for 30% less than a new candle, with almost no packaging.

We refill any scent into any vessel, even the one with the slightly-too-dark pour. Seven months in and we’ve now re-poured more than 2,400 vessels.
