The scent library

Seven signatures.
Nothing else.

We could have launched with twenty scents. We didn’t. Every Saltbrush fragrance is built from Australian-grown essential oils and natural absolutes, developed over months, and cured before it goes near a wick. Here’s each of them — with notes, mood and the rooms they were quietly designed for.

Layering

Two scents, one house.

Most Saltbrush regulars run two scents at a time — one grounding, one bright. Try Saltbrush After Rain in the living room with Hahndorf Morning in the kitchen, or Ironbark & Smoke up against Linen Line at the other end of the house. It’s quiet, domestic, and genuinely your own.
