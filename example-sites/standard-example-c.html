About Golden Crust

Golden Crust Bakery has been serving the Narooma community since 2012 with authentic, freshly baked artisan breads, croissants, and pastries. We bake everything from scratch daily using premium ingredients and traditional techniques. Our cozy cafe is the perfect spot to enjoy your favourite pastry with a freshly brewed coffee.
