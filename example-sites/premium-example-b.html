Welcome to Salt & Swell

Since 2015, Salt & Swell has been teaching surfers of all ages and abilities how to catch waves and master the ocean. Located right on the pristine beaches of Ulladulla, our experienced instructors provide personalized coaching in a safe, supportive environment. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to improve your technique, we have the perfect lesson for you.
