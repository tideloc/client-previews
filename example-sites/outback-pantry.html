From the Bush to Your Pantry

Outback Pantry started in 2020 when Sarah and Tom began experimenting with native ingredients growing wild on their Mudgee property. Lemon myrtle, wattleseed, bush tomato — plants used for thousands of years by First Nations people.

What began as kitchen experiments became weekend markets, then online orders, then a commercial kitchen. Every product is still small-batch, with ingredients from ethical suppliers and Indigenous-owned businesses.

We make beautiful pantry staples that introduce people to the incredible flavours of the Australian bush — one jar at a time.
