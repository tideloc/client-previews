Our Story

Kings Eyewear was founded in 1989 with a simple mission: to bring world-class optical care to the communities of Warrawong and Shellharbour. Three decades later, we remain fiercely independent—never a chain, never compromising on quality or service.

Every member of our team is dedicated to understanding your unique vision needs. We believe in building relationships, not just transactions. Your eyes deserve the personal attention and expertise that only a truly local, independent optical practice can provide.

From frame selection to precise prescription fitting, we handle every detail with care. We stock carefully curated collections that reflect both timeless elegance and contemporary trends—because great eyewear is personal.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my eyes tested? +

We recommend comprehensive eye tests every 1-2 years for most adults. If you have existing eye conditions or wear contact lenses, annual testing is ideal. Children should be tested annually to monitor visual development.

Can I order eyewear online? +

Yes. Once you have your current prescription, you can browse and order from our curated collection online. We ship Australia-wide with tracking. For frame sizing advice, our team is happy to help via phone or email.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfer. Online and in-store purchases both accept secure payment options for your convenience.

Do you offer adjustments and repairs? +

Absolutely. We provide professional frame adjustments, hinge repairs, lens replacement, and general maintenance. Bring your glasses in and we'll get them looking perfect again.

What are your store hours? +

We're open Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM, and Saturday, 8:30 AM – 12:30 PM. Both Warrawong and Shellharbour locations operate on the same schedule. Sunday: Closed.

Can I book an appointment? +

Yes. We welcome walk-ins, but we also accommodate appointments to minimize wait times. Call us to book your eye test or frame fitting at your preferred location.
