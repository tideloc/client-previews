Our Story

For 37 years, Kings Eyewear has remained a cornerstone of eye care excellence in the NSW South Coast. What started as a commitment to personalised optical service has grown into a trusted family business serving thousands of satisfied customers across Warrawong and Shellharbour.

We believe in the power of expert care, premium frames, and genuine relationships. Every customer who walks through our door is treated as a valued member of our family. We stay independent to maintain the values that matter: honest advice, quality eyewear, and the kind of local service that builds loyalty across generations.

From comprehensive eye tests to bespoke frame fitting, we're dedicated to helping you see—and look—your absolute best.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have an eye test?
We recommend a comprehensive eye test every two years for adults, or annually if you wear prescription lenses or have specific eye conditions. Children should be tested annually.
Do you offer online shopping and shipping?
Yes, we ship orders Australia-wide. Visit our online shop to browse frames and lenses, or contact us for personalised recommendations delivered to your home.
Can I get my prescription filled from another optometrist?
Absolutely. We welcome prescriptions from any qualified optometrist or eye care professional. Bring your prescription, and we'll help you find the perfect frames and lenses.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, card, and online payments. Flexible payment options are also available for larger purchases—just ask our team.
Do you offer eye care services for children?
Yes, we specialise in paediatric eye care with patient, expert testing and fun frame selections tailored to younger patients.
What brands do you stock?
We carry a curated selection of premium and designer brands. Visit our locations or browse our shop online to see our full range.
