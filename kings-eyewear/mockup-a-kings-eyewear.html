Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about eye care and our services.

How often should I get my eyes tested? +

We recommend a comprehensive eye test every 1–2 years for most adults. If you have existing conditions or risk factors, more frequent visits may be beneficial. Our optometrists will advise you on the best schedule for your individual needs.

Do you accept health fund rebates? +

Yes! We accept most major health funds and can process claims on the spot. Bring your health fund membership card to your appointment, and we'll handle the billing for you.

Can I order glasses online? +

Yes, we offer an online shop where you can browse and purchase eyewear. For prescription glasses, we'll need your current prescription. We can ship to most locations or arrange in-store pickup at either our Warrawong or Shellharbour store.

Do you offer contact lens fittings? +

Absolutely! We provide professional contact lens fittings with thorough instruction and aftercare support. Our optometrists will ensure your lenses are perfectly fitted for comfort and vision quality.

What if I have a problem with my glasses? +

We offer comprehensive repair and adjustment services. Bring your glasses into either store, and our team will assess the issue. Most repairs are completed quickly, often while you wait.
